Website Report

  1. We have created 5 separate pages for the website. The home page, events page, membership page, leadership page, and about page which can be navigated through the use of our navigation bar.
  2. An original "Chess Club" favicon is in use for the website
  3. Our website has a header with a logo and a navigation bar.
  4. Our website uses a navigation bar at the top of the screen to allow users to navigate the website
  5. Our website works well on different screen sizes and is responsive to different screen sizes through the use of media queries
  6. Our website uses images and includes alt text for each image
  7. Every page of the website has been validated by the Nu Validator and the WAVE Accessibility Evaluator
  8. Our website uses two different types of font faces, Inter and Gill Sans
  9. Our website has a form where users can submit their name and email address to be "added" to our interest list
  10. Our website has a link to a couple of different external sites such as the JMU Chess Club Facebook page and the JMU Chess Club Instagram page
