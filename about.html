JMU Chess Club

About Us

Welcome to the James Madison University Chess Club! We are a group of chess enthusiasts who love to play, learn, and share our passion for the game. Our club is open to JMU students, faculty, and staff, as well as chess players from the Harrisonburg community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote the game of chess on campus and in the community, provide opportunities for players of all skill levels to improve their game, and foster a sense of community among our members. We believe that chess is more than just a game - it's a way to exercise your mind, develop critical thinking skills, and build friendships.

Our Activities

We meet regularly throughout the academic year to play chess, learn new strategies and techniques, and have fun! Some of our activities include:

  • Casual play: We meet weekly to play casual games of chess and socialize with other members.
  • Tournaments: We organize and participate in local and regional chess tournaments.
  • Workshops: We host workshops and lectures on various aspects of the game, such as opening theory, endgame strategy, and tactics.
